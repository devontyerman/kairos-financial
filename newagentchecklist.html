1

Apply for your state license & submit your carrier contracts

Start immediately Work with Allie

First, apply for your state license. Then work with Allie to get every carrier contract completed and submitted in full. This is the one thing that can hold your entire launch up, so push to finish it as fast as you can. You don't have to wait for full approval to keep moving — start the next steps in parallel — but stay on this until Allie confirms it's all in.

You won't be able to submit your carrier contracts until your NPN number comes back. As soon as you have it, come back to this step and complete them — and let Allie know the moment you get your NPN.

2

Apply for your nonresident licenses

Needs your NPN

As soon as you have your NPN number, apply for 4–6 additional state licenses. Step-by-step instructions are in the Sales Hub.

Just submitted your resident license? Your NPN usually takes a few days to a week to come back, and you can't purchase licenses until then. Come back to this step once you have it — for now, keep moving through the rest of the steps.

3

Get your equipment ready

Before you start

Set yourself up to work effectively. You'll need a laptop, a stable Wi-Fi connection, and a quiet place to work where you can take calls without interruptions.

For your headset, we recommend the Jabra Evolve2 Flex — the audio quality is well worth it. If budget is tight, you can start with the more affordable option below, but plan to upgrade to the Jabra.

4

Register for Discord

Community

Get connected with the team. If you don't already have a Discord account, create one — this is where day-to-day communication, questions, and announcements happen, so set it up early.

The invite to join our Discord server is in the Sales Hub — but you'll need a Discord account before you can register for the Sales Hub, so create your account here first.

New to Discord and not sure how to use it? Watch this quick tutorial below.

5

Register for the Sales Hub & complete the training

Start immediately

Create your Sales Hub account and work through every training video, start to finish. Everything else lives here, so get in right away and don't skip ahead or skim.

Open the Sales Hub
6

Set up your CRM

In the Sales Hub

Get your CRM configured so you're ready to dial. The full setup walkthrough and your onboarding details both live inside the Sales Training Hub — follow the steps there.

Find this inside the Sales Training Hub
7

Memorize the script & objections — then drill them

Practice daily

Learn the sales script and the objection responses cold. Rehearse them out loud, over and over, until they're automatic and still sound natural — so you can stop thinking about the words and focus on the person.

Script & objections are in the Sales Training Hub
8

Shadow live presentations & review pre-recorded sales

Learn by watching

Sit in on agents presenting live, and listen back to pre-recorded sales to hear how the script holds up in real conversations — where top performers slow down, adapt, and close.

Pre-recorded sales are in the Sales Training Hub
9

Log your numbers & set up your carrier portals

Ongoing

Head to your profile page in the Sales Hub and log all of your writing numbers and state license numbers there. Then, as carrier approval emails land, register and log in to each carrier portal right away — and bookmark every portal in one dedicated folder (Google Chrome works best) so they're all in one place.

Writing & state license numbers logged on your Sales Hub profile
Each carrier portal registered as the approval email arrives
All portals bookmarked in one Chrome folder
10

Attend your new agent orientation

Live session

Show up to your new agent orientation. This is where everything you've set up comes together. You'll learn how the day-to-day runs, and get clear on what's expected of you out of the gate.

Reach out to your mentor for the date of the next new agent orientation call.

11

Launch call with your mentor

Fill out the Launch Plan first

Your final step before go-live. On this call you'll sit down with your mentor to talk through your personal goals and build a lead plan custom-tailored to your situation. Before the call, fill out your launch plan sheet linked below. Don't fill it out on your computer — print it out and keep it on your desk.
